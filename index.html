~~Filipino Recipes~~

========================================================================

Chicken Adobo:

Filipino Chicken Adobo is a classic comfort dish made by braising chicken in a savory,

tangy sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. Slow-cooked until

tender and deeply flavorful, it’s known for its rich aroma, balance of salty and sour,

and irresistible sauce that’s perfect over steamed rice.

========================================================================

Tortang Talong:

Tortang Talong is a beloved Filipino dish made from grilled eggplant that’s peeled, flattened,

dipped in egg, and pan-fried until golden. It’s smoky, soft on the inside, slightly crispy

on the outside, and often enjoyed with rice and banana ketchup or a savory dipping sauce.

========================================================================

Bulalo:

Bulalo is a hearty Filipino beef soup made by slow-simmering beef shanks and marrow bones

until the broth turns rich and flavorful. Loaded with tender beef, corn, cabbage, and

potatoes, it’s comforting, savory, and best enjoyed hot—especially on a cool day.
