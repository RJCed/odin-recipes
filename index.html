~~Chicken Adobo~~

Filipino Chicken Adobo is a classic comfort dish made by braising chicken in a savory, tangy sauce of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. Slow-cooked until tender and deeply flavorful, it’s known for its rich aroma, balance of salty and sour, and irresistible sauce that’s perfect over steamed rice.

Bulalo:

Bulalo is a hearty Filipino beef soup made by slow-simmering beef shanks and marrow bones

until the broth turns rich and flavorful. Loaded with tender beef, corn, cabbage, and

potatoes, it’s comforting, savory, and best enjoyed hot—especially on a cool day.
